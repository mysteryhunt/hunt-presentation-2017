sl 1 wyif-p1-psso with yarn in front, slip 1 stitch from the left-hand needle to the right-hand needle without working the stitch; purl 1; pass slipped stitch over the stitch just worked and off right-hand needle
sl 1-k2 tog-psso slip 1 stitch; knit 2 stitches together as one stitch; pass slipped stitch over the stitch just worked and off right-hand needle
ssk (slip, slip, knit): slip 2 stitches knitwise, return slipped stitches to left needle, knit 2 together through back loop
yo (yarn over): take the yarn over the top of the needle once before making the next stitch. If the next stitch is to be knitted, simply take yarn over the top of the right-hand needle to the back. If the next stitch is to be purled, take yarn over the top of the right-hand needle and then under the needle to the front.
(yo) twice (double yarn-over): take the yarn twice over the top of the right-hand needle before making the next stitch, i.e., over the needle to the back, under the needle to the front, over to the back again to position to knit the next stitch. If the next stitch is to be purled, the yarn is brought under the needle to the front once more to purl position.
