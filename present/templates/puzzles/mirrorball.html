Rules of Mirrorball

In each 5x5 array in the playing field, five players, in jerseys numbered 1 through 5, select a white non-goal (goals are shaded gray) square to stand in. Each player then places one angled mirror in a horizontally or vertically adjacent square (which can be a goal), with no two players selecting the same square. Light beam paths that pass through one or more goals score the total of all jersey numbers they pass through. Missed points for beam paths that pass through no goals are provided, along with path length, for reference.
