As we learned in 2008, Chuckie Chicken has had a long and distinguished career in the entertainment field, even aside from his time as Burger King’s Subservient Chicken. In particular, the grace and artistry of his dancing simply could not be matched.

Or . . . could it??

As it turns out, one of Chuckie’s closest friends is MIT’s own Tim the Beaver, who is no slouch in the moves department himself. The two of them competed in dance competitions on many occasions. As in the 2008 videos, these performances no longer exist in their entirety. Sadly the audio was lost, but Chuckie kindly volunteered to record the audio for us.

Enjoy, once again, the charisma and talent of Chuckie Chicken!
