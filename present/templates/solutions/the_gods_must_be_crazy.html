Each of these is a mostly straightforward clue to a word, though in each set of clues the final clue is highlighted/bolded to indicate that it is different than the other clues in the set. If you take the answers to the clues, and sound them out together, you get the name of a demigod from the original Percy Jackson and the Olympians set of books. The final bolded clue is always at word that begins with a Greek numerical prefix. You can then use that number to index into the god parent that that demigod has. This sets are already in the needed order.
