The puzzle's title CHINA KILNS anagrams word-wise to CHAIN LINKS, suggesting that anagramming and creating a chain of some kind are involved.

Each of the phrases is a clue for an answer that solvers need to enter in the blanks to its right. (These answers do not always have "dictionary nature".) The clues are ordered such that their answers are alphabetical. Each answer has one red blank, selecting a single letter, with some letters being shifted in alphabetic position when marked with small signed integers below.
