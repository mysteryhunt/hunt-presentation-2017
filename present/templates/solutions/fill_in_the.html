Each trivia clue contains blanks which can be filled in with words that start with the same letter, and the 25 sets of blanks include all of the alphabet except for the letter G.

Answers to the trivia can be sorted alphabetically by these clue blank letters, and the initital letters of these answers, in this order, spell a final clue. Three of the answers are the word “BLANK”, and these indicate a blank ( ________ ) in the final clue.

Reading the first letters of the answer words, plus the blanks, gives the final clue:

________ ________ OF KHAZARIA IN THE ________ CIV FIVE

Using the hitherto unused G, solvers need to fill this final clue in to get:

GREAT GENERAL OF KHAZARIA IN THE GAME CIV FIVE

(Searching for “Khazaria” and “Civilization V” will reveal the existence of a user-created mod to the game, in which this is the only alliterative GG phrase.) The puzzle’s answer is KHAGAN BEK.
