The five puzzles at stage left begin with the letters A through E, and the five puzzles at stage right end in A through E, suggesting that the the answers can be paired. Doing so, in each pair of answers the name of a Shakespeare can be read across the pair, with one letter omitted. The omitted letters spell the answer, I AM BI—a fitting trait for an actor able to play both men and women with ease.
