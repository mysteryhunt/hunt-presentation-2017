For the most part, the crossword clues give definitions for the down entries, in random order.

  • Sitcom role for Van Dyke: PETRIE
  • Like contact lens solution: SALINE
  • Nike competitor: AVIA
  • Ratatouille vegetable: TOMATO
  • Had ratatouille, maybe: ATE
  • Ripen: AGE
  • Leave It to Beaver exclamation: GEE
  • Shot from a stick, sometimes: SELFIE
  • Spam containers: TINS
  • Defendant in a 1925 trial: SCOPES
  • Pornography: SMUT
  • Negative conjunction: NOR
  • Spigot: TAP
  • Place to put in a PIN: ATM
  • Wiped clean: ERASED
  • Terrier of whodunits: ASTA
  • She played Sally in Cabaret: LIZA
  • Queensland tree-dweller: KOALA
  • Discontinuity: GAP
  • She might get sheared: EWE
  • Maidenform product: BRA
  • Underhanded: SLY

However, in 15 cases (one in each column of the puzzle), the clue is for an entry that is one letter off:

  • Doctor Zhivago role: LARA, not LAMA
  • Candidate’s goal: SEAT, not SWAT
  • Cold remedy, supposedly: CHICKEN SOUP, not CHICKENS OUT
  • Handy identification?: FINGERPRINT, not FINGERPAINT
  • Calms down: QUIETS, not QUILTS
  • Small key: ISLET, not INLET
  • Grain for grinding: GRIST, not GRIFT
  • Oliver Twist’s request: MORE, not PORE
  • The Waltons actor Will: GEER, not GEAR
  • Resident of a certain European capital: STOCKHOLMER, not STOCKHOLDER
  • Bad engine sound: KNOCK, not KNACK
  • Takes the helm: STEERS, not STEEDS
  • Shameless, in a way: UNINHIBITED, not UNINHABITED
  • Book before Joel: HOSEA, not HOSTA
  • Quaker product: OATS, not OATH

Taking the correct letters in clue order, rather than the letters in the grid, spells the answer REPRESS MEMORIES.
