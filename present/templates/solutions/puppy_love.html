Match the pictures of 80s bands to the pictures of their lead singers' last names, then take the given letter of each band name to spell "ANSWER: BASSET", which gives the answer: BASSET.

Unnumbered cards, in order:

A Flock Of Seagulls, ABC, Blondie, Erasure, Europe, Heart, Journey, Men at Work, Queen, Styx, The Cure, Whitesnake

Numbered cards, in order:

Fry, Perry, Coverdale, Hay, Smith, Tempest, Harry, Wilson, Score, Bell, Mercury, DeYoung
