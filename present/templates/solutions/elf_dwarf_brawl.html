This puzzle is a reference to the popular “East/West Bowl” sketch from the show Key and Peele. In the puzzle version, each contestant’s name (except for a couple special ones at the end) contains the name of a mascot and/or nickname of a particular college sports team. Those colleges (or other institutions) are the ones referenced in the original K&P version. That reference links each character in the puzzle version to a specific character in the K&P video. The “LEVEL” of each puzzle character gives an index into that K&P character.

The tracking of puzzle characters to characters in the original video, and extracted letters, are in the table below. The final result, SEND TWO CLIPS USE MIT AND OLIN COLLEGE, tells you to make similar video clips with characters incorporating the mascots and/or nicknames of those institutions (Tim the Beaver/Engineer, Frank/Phoenix). When those are submitted, the team will receive the final answer for the puzzle, SEAN LENNON.
