790.06 is not, in fact, the Dewey Decimal code for crossword puzzles. Rather, it is the number of Florida's concealed weapons statute. The names of nine weapons are concealed in the grid, crossing one grey square each. The letters in those grey squares spell RAPIER WIT, the answer to this puzzle.
