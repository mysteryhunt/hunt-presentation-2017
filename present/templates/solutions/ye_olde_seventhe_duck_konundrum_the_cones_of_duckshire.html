After completing the game (which takes 25 turns before the Ledgerman wins 4-2-2-2), the instructions indicate that the players, in seat order, should look at the area in front of their cones from left to right. For each cone, there is one letter taking up most of the space in front of the cone from the owner's vantage point:

  • Arbiter (Seat 1) - FLP cone - L
  • Arbiter (Seat 1) - IJX cone - I
  • Maverick (Seat 3) - GRW cone - G
  • Maverick (Seat 3) - EHN cone - H
  • Ledgerman (Seat 6) - ETV cone - T
  • Ledgerman (Seat 6) - HKY cone - H
  • Ledgerman (Seat 6) - ACO cone - O
  • Ledgerman (Seat 6) - MPU cone - U
  • Corporal (Seat 8) - APS cone - S
  • Corporal (Seat 8) - CEI cone - E

Thus, the answer is LIGHTHOUSE. Below are two tables showing the token placements and actions taken in each turn, and a running account of each player's seat, tokens on the board, cones on the board, and dollarbucks after each turn.
